## 한국어 (Korean)

### 1. 개인정보의 수집 및 이용 목적

**페이퍼토이 드레스업 게임**(이하 "본 앱")은 사용자의 개인정보를 최소한으로 수집하며, 다음의 목적으로만 사용합니다:

#### 수집하는 정보
본 앱은 **개인 식별 정보를 수집하지 않습니다**. 다음의 정보만 자동으로 수집됩니다:

1. **앱 사용 통계 (Firebase Analytics)**
   - 앱 실행 횟수
   - 화면 전환 기록
   - 기기 정보 (OS 버전, 기기 모델, 화면 크기)
   - 국가 및 언어 설정
   - **목적:** 앱 성능 개선 및 사용자 경험 향상

2. **앱 오류 정보 (Firebase Crashlytics)**
   - 앱 충돌 및 오류 로그
   - 스택 트레이스 (코드 실행 경로)
   - 기기 정보 및 OS 버전
   - **목적:** 앱 안정성 개선 및 버그 수정

3. **광고 식별자 (Google AdMob)**
   - 광고 ID (AAID/IDFA)
   - 광고 노출 및 클릭 이벤트
   - **목적:** 맞춤형 광고 제공 (사용자는 설정에서 광고 추적을 거부할 수 있습니다)

#### 수집하지 않는 정보
- ❌ 이름, 이메일, 전화번호 등 개인 식별 정보
- ❌ 위치 정보 (GPS, IP 주소)
- ❌ 연락처, 사진 등 민감한 개인정보
- ❌ 결제 정보 (본 앱은 무료이며 인앱 결제가 없습니다)

### 2. 권한 사용 목적

본 앱은 다음 권한을 요청합니다:

#### Android
- **저장소 접근 권한** (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)
  - **목적:** 사용자가 제작한 페이퍼토이 이미지를 기기의 갤러리에 저장
  - **사용 방식:** 사용자가 "저장하기" 버튼을 눌렀을 때만 실행
  - **철회 방법:** 기기 설정 > 앱 > 페이퍼토이 드레스업 > 권한에서 언제든지 취소 가능

#### iOS
- **사진 라이브러리 추가 권한** (NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription)
  - **목적:** 페이퍼토이 이미지를 사진 앱에 저장
  - **사용 방식:** 저장 시에만 요청
  - **철회 방법:** 설정 > 페이퍼토이 드레스업 > 사진에서 취소 가능

## English

### 1. Purpose of Personal Information Collection and Use

**Paper Toy Dress Up Game** (hereinafter "the App") collects minimal user information and uses it only for the following purposes:

#### Information We Collect
The App **does not collect personally identifiable information**. Only the following information is automatically collected:

1. **App Usage Statistics (Firebase Analytics)**
   - Number of app launches
   - Screen navigation history
   - Device information (OS version, device model, screen size)
   - Country and language settings
   - **Purpose:** Improve app performance and user experience

2. **App Error Information (Firebase Crashlytics)**
   - App crash and error logs
   - Stack traces (code execution paths)
   - Device information and OS version
   - **Purpose:** Improve app stability and fix bugs

3. **Advertising Identifier (Google AdMob)**
   - Advertising ID (AAID/IDFA)
   - Ad impression and click events
   - **Purpose:** Provide personalized ads (users can opt out of ad tracking in settings)

#### Information We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ Location data (GPS, IP address)
- ❌ Sensitive personal information (contacts, photos)
- ❌ Payment information (the App is free with no in-app purchases)

### 2. Permissions Usage

The App requests the following permissions:

#### Android
- **Storage Access** (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)
  - **Purpose:** Save paper toy images created by users to device gallery
  - **Usage:** Only when user presses "Save" button
  - **Revocation:** Settings > Apps > Paper Toy Dress Up > Permissions

#### iOS
- **Photo Library Add Permission** (NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription)
  - **Purpose:** Save paper toy images to Photos app
  - **Usage:** Requested only when saving
  - **Revocation:** Settings > Paper Toy Dress Up > Photos

### 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

#### Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)
- **Data Shared:** App usage statistics, error logs
- **Purpose:** App performance analysis and stability improvement
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

#### Google AdMob
- **Data Shared:** Advertising ID, ad events
- **Purpose:** Ad revenue to support free app operation
- **Privacy Policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Opt-out of Personalized Ads:** [https://www.google.com/settings/ads](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)

### 4. Data Retention Period

- **Firebase Analytics/Crashlytics:** Stored for up to 2 months per Google's data retention policy, then automatically deleted
- **Local Data:** All data is immediately deleted when the app is uninstalled

### 5. Data Deletion

- All data stored on the device is automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled
- Analytics data collected by Firebase is automatically deleted per Google's retention policy

6. Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliance)
This app is designed for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

We prioritize the protection of children's privacy and adhere to the following:
* This app does not require account registration and does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, addresses, or phone numbers from children.
* Data created within the app (such as character designs) is stored solely on the user's device and is not transmitted to our servers.
* If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns regarding your child's data, please contact us at the email provided below, and we will take immediate action.

### 7. User Rights

Users have the following rights:
- **Right to Access and Correction:** Firebase data is collected anonymously and cannot be individually accessed
- **Right to Deletion:** All local data is deleted when the app is uninstalled
- **Right to Data Processing Restriction:** You can stop data collection by discontinuing app use or uninstalling

This policy may be modified according to relevant laws and service changes:

## 日本語 (Japanese)

### 1. 個人情報の収集および利用目的

**ペーパートイドレスアップゲーム**（以下「本アプリ」）は、ユーザーの個人情報を最小限に収集し、以下の目的でのみ使用します。

#### 収集する情報
本アプリは**個人を特定できる情報を収集しません**。以下の情報のみが自動的に収集されます：

1. **アプリ使用統計 (Firebase Analytics)**
   - アプリ起動回数
   - 画面遷移記録
   - デバイス情報（OSバージョン、デバイスモデル、画面サイズ）
   - 国および言語設定
   - **目的:** アプリのパフォーマンス改善とユーザー体験向上

2. **アプリエラー情報 (Firebase Crashlytics)**
   - アプリのクラッシュおよびエラーログ
   - スタックトレース（コード実行パス）
   - デバイス情報およびOSバージョン
   - **目的:** アプリの安定性改善とバグ修正

3. **広告識別子 (Google AdMob)**
   - 広告ID (AAID/IDFA)
   - 広告表示およびクリックイベント
   - **目的:** カスタマイズされた広告の提供（ユーザーは設定で広告トラッキングを拒否できます）

#### 収集しない情報
- ❌ 氏名、メール、電話番号などの個人識別情報
- ❌ 位置情報（GPS、IPアドレス）
- ❌ 連絡先、写真などの機密個人情報
- ❌ 決済情報（本アプリは無料でアプリ内課金はありません）

### 2. 権限の使用目的

本アプリは以下の権限を要求します：

#### Android
- **ストレージアクセス権限** (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)
  - **目的:** ユーザーが作成したペーパートイ画像をデバイスのギャラリーに保存
  - **使用方法:** ユーザーが「保存」ボタンを押したときのみ実行
  - **取り消し方法:** 設定 > アプリ > ペーパートイドレスアップ > 権限からいつでも取り消し可能

#### iOS
- **写真ライブラリ追加権限** (NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription)
  - **目的:** ペーパートイ画像を写真アプリに保存
  - **使用方法:** 保存時のみ要求
  - **取り消し方法:** 設定 > ペーパートイドレスアップ > 写真から取り消し可能

本アプリは以下の第三者サービスを使用します：

- **プライバシーポリシー:** [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
